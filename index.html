Why Now

AI Is Rewriting the Physical Limits of the Data Center

AI compute is driving a step change in rack density. Current rack-scale systems are already at 120 kW, and the next deployment class of 250 to 500 kW racks is already being planned. That shift is forcing a rethink of how data centers are physically designed, powered, cooled, and operated.
